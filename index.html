Scope of the
Conference

The Department of Physics is organizing the 6th National Conference on "Advanced Materials and Radiation Physics" (AMRP2023) on the pattern of previous conferences organized by the department in the year 2009, 2011, 2013, 2015 and 2020. Proceedings of our previous conferences (AMRP-2015) and (AMRP-2020) were published in AIP Conf. Proceedings bearing vol. no. 1675 and 2352 respectively.

All Information Related to the Conference

Mode of Presentation

AMRP-2023 employs hybrid mode of presentation. A keynote lecture, invited talks, and contributed (oral and poster) presentations will be used to present papers in physical sessions (desktop sessions with all necessary facilities) as well as online using video conferencing and "Google-meet" platform.

Aim and Scope

This conference offers an excellent forum and a chance to discuss research concepts with distinguished academics & scientists working in the area of Material Science and Radiation Physics. The scope of the conference includes advances in advanced materials and Radiation Physics.

Best Paper Award

Recognizing and awarding researchers for their excellent work can lead to increased visibility, credibility, funding, collaboration, and career advancement opportunities, all of which contribute to their status as experts in their field and also and helps to advance scientific research.

Registration Fee Receipt

Registration fee receipt will be provided upon completion of registration for delegates attending the conference. This conference promises to be a great platform for scientists, researchers, and students from all over the country to network and exchange ideas about advanced materials and radiation physics.

Digital Certificate

Registrants for this conference will receive a digital certificate. This certificate will be useful in demonstrating your expertise and knowledge in advanced materials and radiation physics. This certificate will also help participants find work or academic positions in this field.

Conference Tour

"Conference tours for Amritsar (Golden Temple, Wagha Border, about 225 km from Longowal) and Chandigarh (Rock Garden, Sukhna Lake, Rose Garden, Pinjore Garden, about 150 km from Longowal) will be arranged for delegates on a payment basis on May 20, 2020. Delegates can sign up for these tours to explore the cultural and scenic highlights of Amritsar and Chandigarh. The payment basis ensures that only interested individuals get to enjoy these experiences."
